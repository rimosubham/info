01.6 Usage rules
— Do —
  • Keep "Peak" in Clay (#A85438) on light grounds.
  • Maintain clear space equal to one cap-height of "O" all around.
  • Pair with a single tagline in IBM Plex Mono — or use no tagline at all.
  • Use the monogram "OP" for any square or sub-32px application.
  • Keep tracking at +0.02em for the wordmark.
— Don't —
  • Never bold, italicise, or outline the wordmark.
  • Never colour the whole wordmark in a single colour other than the three approved.
  • Never place over busy imagery without a solid block or 40%+ overlay behind it.
  • Never stretch, skew, rotate, or add drop shadows.
  • Never use tagline under 11px — it becomes illegible.

02.6 Usage rules
— Do —
  • Always keep "Peak" in italic Instrument Serif — the italic is the signature.
  • Hold Fraunces at weight 200 for the wordmark. Heavier weights break the quiet.
  • Maintain 1.5× cap-height of clear space around the mark.
  • Use Sand for tagline dots only — nowhere else within the wordmark.
  • Use the "op." monogram with the lowercase period included. It is part of the mark.
— Don't —
  • Never bold the wordmark. Never use Fraunces above weight 400.
  • Never remove the italic on "Peak" — the whole mark depends on it.
  • Never use the wordmark in Sand or Jade — both lack contrast on Ivory.
  • Never stack the two words vertically. Always horizontal.
  • Never place over photography without a 50%+ dark overlay underneath.
