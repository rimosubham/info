Where we're based

A wooden house in Tirthan Valley.

Our HQ — and we use the word loosely — is a two-story wooden house in Gushaini, Tirthan Valley. 4 hours from Chandigarh, 30 minutes from the last cell tower. No dress code. Excellent chai.

If you're passing through, we genuinely love meeting travelers in person. Drop a WhatsApp first so we can put the kettle on — the local kind, not the electric one.

Postal address (for legal things and surprise packages):
